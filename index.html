About Lebanese Cuisine

Lebanese cuisine is a celebration of flavors, colors, and textures, known for its variety of dishes that incorporate fresh ingredients, herbs, and spices.

Featured Dishes

Tabbouleh

A refreshing parsley and bulgur salad with tomatoes and lemon dressing.

Hummus

Creamy chickpea dip blended with tahini, garlic, and lemon juice.

Kibbeh

A savory mixture of bulgur, minced onions, and finely ground lean beef, lamb, goat, or camel meat.
